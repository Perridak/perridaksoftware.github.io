Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Perridak Software operates under a privacy‑first design philosophy. Our systems and products are intentionally created so that we do not collect, store, or retain personal information about our customers unless you choose to provide it for a specific and temporary support interaction.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we handle, how we handle it, and how third‑party services (such as Stripe) may process personal data on our behalf.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not automatically collect or store:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Payment details
  • IP addresses (beyond transient technical logs)
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device identifiers
  • Any other personal or identifying information

Our software and websites are designed so that personal data is never required to use our products. For our apps, any personal data you choose to store within them exists only within the app database in the devices and cloud services that you control, and cannot be transmitted without your express permission.

2. Information You Choose to Provide

If you contact us for support, you may choose to share information such as:

  • A support request description
  • Diagnostic details
  • Software licence keys
  • Screenshots or logs

This information is used only for the purpose of resolving your support request.

Once the support case is closed, we delete or redact any personal details or licence keys from our systems. We do not retain this information for any other purpose.

3. Payment Processing (Stripe)

We use Stripe to process payments for our products.

When you make a purchase, all personal and payment information is handled directly by Stripe, not by Perridak Software.

Stripe may collect:

  • name
  • email address
  • billing address
  • payment method details
  • device and browser information
  • IP address
  • fraud‑prevention metadata

Stripe processes this information in accordance with: -the Australian Privacy Act -the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) -the UK GDPR -other applicable laws

Stripe acts as a data processor for payment information and a data controller for fraud‑prevention and regulatory data.

You can read Stripe’s privacy policy here: https://stripe.com/privacy

Perridak Software never receives or stores your payment details.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Because we do not store personal data, our GDPR obligations are minimal. Where processing occurs through Stripe, the lawful bases are:

  • Contract — processing necessary to complete your purchase
  • Legitimate interest — fraud prevention and secure payment handling

We do not perform profiling, analytics, or automated decision‑making.

5. Third‑Party Services

Some services necessary for operating our business are provided by third parties. These may include:

  • Issue tracking platforms (e.g., GitHub)
  • Payment processors (e.g., Stripe)
  • App stores
  • Email or communication providers

These services may collect personal information as part of their normal operation.
We do not control, store, or access the personal information collected by these providers, and any privacy or “right to be forgotten” requests must be directed to them.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third‑party service you interact with.

6. Your Rights (GDPR, UK GDPR, CCPA, etc.)

Because Perridak Software does not store personal information, most privacy rights requests (access, correction, deletion, portability) do not apply to us.

If you have shared personal information with a third‑party service (e.g., GitHub, payment processors, app stores), you may exercise your privacy rights directly with those providers, including:

  • Access to your data
  • Correction
  • Deletion / right to be forgotten
  • Restriction or objection to processing

Since we do not store personal information, and we do not own or operate 3rd-party systems, we cannot action these requests on your behalf. If you contact us regarding data we do not hold, we will confirm that no personal information is stored.

7. Payment Information

We do not process or store payment details.
All transactions are handled by secure third‑party payment processors or app stores, which may collect information required to complete the transaction. Any sales information is reduced to metadata indicating the nature of the sale without storing your personal details. Such metadata is limited by design to enable us to assist you with an enquiry if you choose to share information from your apps that can be linked to your purchases, and the metadata cannot be reverse engineered to identify you.

8. Legal Compliance

Although we do not retain personal information, we operate in accordance with:

  • The Australian Privacy Act 1988 and its most recent amendments
  • The Australian Privacy Principles (APPs)
  • The EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for international customers

Because we do not collect or store personal data, many obligations under these laws do not apply to us. However, we remain committed to transparency, minimal data handling, and privacy‑by‑design.
