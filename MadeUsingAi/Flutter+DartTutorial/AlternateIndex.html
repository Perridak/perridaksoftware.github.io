For experienced developers making the leap to Dart and Flutter

Welcome

This tutorial is designed for developers with experience in other languages who want to learn Dart and Flutter without starting from square one. We skip the basics and focus on how to accomplish things the Dart and Flutter way.

Work through the chapters in order. Each builds on the previous one, but feel free to skip sections if you're already familiar with a concept.
