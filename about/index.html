About Us

Perridak Software exists to build technology with intention, care, and respect. We create multiplatform apps and developer APIs with a simple guiding belief: quality doesn’t happen by accident. It’s the result of meticulous craftsmanship, thoughtful design, and a commitment to doing things the right way, even when that path is harder or slower. Every line of code we write reflects that philosophy.

We are agile in the truest sense of the word — adaptable, resilient, and unafraid of uncertainty. Challenges don’t deter us; they energise us. We embrace the unknown with audacity and curiosity, approaching each project as an opportunity to learn, refine, and push ourselves further. That mindset shapes not only how we build software, but how we operate day-to-day.

Integrity is a cornerstone of who we are. We embrace candour, transparency, and hold ourselves accountable by owning our decisions and learning from our mistakes. Respect is equally central to our ethos: respect for our customers, for our collaborators, and for ourselves. That respect naturally extends to privacy. We design our products so that customer autonomy is preserved, and we do not record any customer data anywhere. Privacy isn’t an afterthought for us; it’s built into both our business and our products by design.

Our most central tenet is simple: Do No Harm. It guides our choices, our policies, and the way we present ourselves to the world. This principle is enshrined in our operating policy in a way that cannot be diluted or quietly rewritten, as it underpins our ethos and our entire identity as a business.

Perridak Software operates on Wadawurrung Country. We therefore acknowledge and pay our respects to the Wadawurrung people, to their Elders past, present and emerging, and to their enduring relationship with the lands and waterways where we are honoured to live and work. The name “Perridak” honours the animal whose qualities we admire and is used with the utmost respect for the people who named it long before colonisation. We see something of ourselves in the Perridak — resilient, purposeful, and deeply connected to the environment it inhabits. That inspiration is woven into our identity and the way we choose to operate.
